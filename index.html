Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I connect multiple properties?

+

You can connect multiple properties through the Multi-Property Dashboard. Navigate to Settings > Properties > Add New Property. Follow the setup wizard to configure each property with its unique settings while maintaining centralized control.

What integrations are available?

+

We support integrations with major OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb), payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, Square), accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), and marketing platforms (Mailchimp, HubSpot). Custom integrations are also available through our API.
