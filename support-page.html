Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Why can't I complete my booking?

+

There could be several reasons why you're unable to complete a booking: the room might no longer be available, there might be an issue with your payment method, or there could be a technical problem. Try refreshing the page, using a different browser, or clearing your cache. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

How do I modify my reservation?

+

You can modify your reservation by logging into the Guest Portal with your email and booking reference. Navigate to "My Bookings," select the reservation you want to modify, and click "Modify Reservation." From there, you can change dates, room types, or add services (subject to availability).

Is my payment information secure?

+

Yes, we take security seriously. All payment information is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

How do I connect to the hostel Wi-Fi?

+

You'll receive Wi-Fi access details upon check-in. Simply connect to the "PVT-Guest" network and enter the password provided. If you're having trouble connecting, please visit the reception desk for assistance.
